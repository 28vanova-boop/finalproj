What is Chemistry?

Chemistry is a branch of science that focuses on the composition, structure, properties, and changes of matter. It helps scientists understand how substances combine and react with one another at the molecular level.

Why is Chemistry Important?

Chemistry plays a major role in everyday life. It is used in medicine to create new drugs, in agriculture to improve food production, and in environmental science to protect natural resources. Without chemistry, many modern technologies and advancements would not be possible.
